# Privacy Policy

**CW Carver - Morse Code Trainer**
**Last updated:** February 13, 2026

## Data Collection

CW Carver does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All app data — including your progress, settings, and session history — is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.

## No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not include any analytics frameworks, crash reporting tools, or advertising SDKs.

## No Network Access

CW Carver does not make any network requests. The app functions entirely offline.

## Data Storage

Your learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not accessible to us or any third party. Uninstalling the app will delete all stored data.

## Children's Privacy

CW Carver does not collect any data from any users, including children.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/BLANXLAIT/morse-trainer/issues

## Changes

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in this document with an updated date.
